We are glad to announce that despite the testing times we have managed to shift techFEST online. techFEST, SLIET is the platform of collaboration where students, innovators, technology enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, changemakers and over 10000 students from all over the country gather to solve interesting problems, share knowledge and compete to win exciting prizes and gain recognition. For over two decades, techFEST has been a spectacular annual technical festival of SLIET (A tier-1 NBA accredited college situated in Longowal, Punjab). Each year, the announcement of techFEST stirs up excitement among students from all over the country. Due to the restrictions imposed by the pandemic, we are doing things differently this year. We hereby invite you to the virtual version of techFEST - techFEST'21. Join us as we take tiny steps to revitalize India through 27+ events, talks and webinars.
